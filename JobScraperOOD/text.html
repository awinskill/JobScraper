WHAT ARE COOKIES?

\n
\n
A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we\n store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer if you\n agree. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your\n computer\'s hard drive.
\n
\n\n

WHAT TYPES OF COOKIES DO WE USE?

\n
\n
We use two types of cookies on our website:
\n
\'Session Cookies\'
These are temporary cookies,\n which only exist in the period you access the website (or more\n strictly, until you close the browser after accessing the website).\n Session cookies help our website remember what you chose on the\n previous page, therefore avoiding having to re-enter information. On\n our website, these cookies do not contain personal information, and\n cannot be used to identify you.
\n
\'Persistent Cookies\'
These are cookies remain on\n your device after you have visited our website. These cookies help\n us to identify you as a unique visitor (by storing a randomly\n generated number).
\n
\n\n
